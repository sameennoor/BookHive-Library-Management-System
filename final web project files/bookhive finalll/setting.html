💡 Help & Website Guide

Welcome to BookHive Library Management System! This guide will help you understand the purpose and usage of each section of the website.

🏠 Dashboard

The main overview page that displays key statistics — such as total books, issued and returned books, memberships, and visitors — in interactive cards and charts.

📚 Books

Here, you can view, add, edit, or delete books in the library. Each record includes the book’s title, author, category, and availability status.

👥 Members

This section maintains a record of all library members. You can add new members, update their details, or remove inactive ones.

📖 Issued Books

Tracks which books are currently issued, along with member names and return dates. Helps you manage and avoid overdue returns efficiently.

⚙️ Settings

Customize your website appearance (light/dark mode), access profile settings, and manage privacy options. You can also navigate to this help page from here.

👤 Profile Settings

Update your personal details such as name, email, phone number, and role. All changes are stored for your next session.

🌙 Theme Switch

Switch between Light Mode and Dark Mode using the toggle in the Settings page. Your theme choice is saved automatically and applied across all pages.

🚪 Logout

Safely exit your dashboard to protect your data. You can log back in anytime.
